The credit

30% Production Credit

The Film Production Services Credit returns 30% of qualified in-state wages and spending. Refundable and nonrefundable components.

Sales tax

100% Sales Tax Credit

A separate income/franchise credit covers the full Wisconsin sales and use tax you pay on qualifying production purchases.

The caps

Up to $1M per project

Per-applicant cap is $1 million. Only $5M available statewide each year, so early applications have a real advantage.

Eligibility

Does your project qualify?

If you're spending money in Wisconsin on something people will watch, it probably does. Minimum is $100k for projects over 30 minutes, $50k for shorter works.

Feature films, scripted TV, unscripted TV, documentaries, short films, and broadcast advertising.
